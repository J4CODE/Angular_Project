What is Angular?
by Łukasz Głuszek

Angular can be used for just about any kind of web application, like Content Management Systems (CMS), e-commerce applications, static websites, design systems, banking websites, and more. It will greatly depend on the type of project's specs, your dev team size, and your product scope.

Angular can be a great choice for many different types of web applications. It offers all the most sought-after features but, most importantly, it's supported by Google and has a vibrant community around the framework.

What is Bootstrap?
from the Bootstrap Docs

Bootstrap is a front-end development framework that enables developers & designers to quickly build fully responsive websites.
